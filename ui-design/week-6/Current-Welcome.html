Welcome

Hello and welcome to Sheldon's Game Shelf. This is a website I am developing for my Master's in Software Development program from Maryville University. It will consist of brief information and reviews for games from my personal collection along with information for upcoming games and about myself.


My board game collection currently consists of about 38 games, not including expansions. It includes deck builders, tableau, and co-op games with player counts ranging from 1-15.
