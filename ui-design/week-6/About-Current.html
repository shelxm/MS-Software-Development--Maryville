About Sheldon's Game Shelf

This site is the submission for my final project for SWDV 620 through Maryville University taught by Professor Rodney W. McGee Jr. I decided to use part of my board game collection and make my own review site including infroamtion such as the publisher, player count, recommended ages, and a link to the respective BGG page. There is also a list of upcoming games I am excited about with information including publisher, player count, recommended ages, estimated retail dates, and relevant links.



About Me
