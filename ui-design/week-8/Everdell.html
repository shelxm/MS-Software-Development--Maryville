Everdell,

Everdell has been around for a bit and I have heard excellent things about it. Furthermore one of my favorite books and tv series are Redwall and the game reminds me of it. It is a tableau resource engine-building game where you create a town of wonderfully drawn woodland creatures. I have not played it though and do not expect to recieve my copy until sometime next year as I did the complete collection.
