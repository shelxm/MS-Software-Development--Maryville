Unstable Unicorns

Unstable Unicorns was one of the first games we bought and has been a group staple ever since. The cards are witty and have a wide variety so games are always different. The goal is to have 7 unicorns on your field (aka stable) and there are spells, upgrade, and downgrade cards (one turns unicorns into otters) to play along with unicorn cards. There are tons of expansions that add new elements so the game has lots of support. Expansions include NSFW, Rainbow Apocalypse, and Dragons to name a few. With so many expansions though the overall deck can get bogged down so I would recommend multiple copies of the base game with one or two expansions mixed in. Overall myself and everyone that we have played with loves Unstable Unicorns and its expansions. We love it so much that any TeeTurtle release is an instant buy (Runes and Regulations, Here to Slay).

Looking for a fun casual game that has hilarious cards? I highly recommend this game.

  • Publisher: Teeturtle
  • Player Count: 2-8 players
  • Ages: 14+
