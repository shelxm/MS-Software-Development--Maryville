Ashes: Rise of the Phoenixborn

Ashes is another game that drew me in because of the amazing artwork. It is a strategic game of rolling dice and using them to play cards and summon creatures. Unfortunately it has fallen to the way-side for my playgroup as it is highly strategic and each player is only allowed one main action and one side action, so games can drag. I also feel that while it can play up to 4 players it is more made for 2 as anything above slows the game down more. A year or so ago the game was "cancelled" but the end of last year or early this year it was "reborn" with rebalanced cards and updated rules. Not sure if the changes would make any difference. We play occassionally but there are many other games we would play over Ashes.

If strategic card games are your thing this may be a game for you. Artwork is amazing and cards are well desgined.

  • Publisher: Plaid Hat Games
  • Player Count: 2-4 players
  • Ages: 14+
