Dinosaur World

Dinosaur World looks like Jurassic Park in board game form, which is my favorite IP. Build your own park, roll dice to determine any casualties from jeep tours, and managing your dinosaurs. What else can I say? Super excited for this game, especially as its predecessor Dinosaur Island was highly rated online. Supposed to start shipping around September.

  • Publisher: Pandasaurus
  • Player count: 1-4 players
  • Ages: 10+
  • Retail Release: Summer/Fall 2021
  • Kickstarter Link: Dinosaur World
