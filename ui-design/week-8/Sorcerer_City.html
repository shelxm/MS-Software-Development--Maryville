Sorcerer City

Sorcerer City seems daunting at first with a lot of setup required, table space necessary, and many different parts to learn. It is a highly strategic tile laying game and the time crunch can be intense during rounds. However, after learning it is extremely fun and rewarding and plays at a surprisingly quick pace due to everyone building at the same time. I personally love playing it and despite friends being skeptical at first they have all been left wanting to play more. I like to equate it to deck-building Carcassone if that means anything.

If you have the space and are willing to learn this is a must have game.

  • Publisher: Druid City Games
  • Player Count: 1-6 players
  • Ages: 14+
