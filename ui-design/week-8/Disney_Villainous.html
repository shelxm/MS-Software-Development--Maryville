Disney Villainous

Villainous is a play group and personal favorite. The different character goals, boards, and decks are thematically on point and the game receives frequent support in expansions. There are about 15 total villains that can be played and the game has expanded into a Marvel version with its own first expansion coming out. Some characters are a little more balanced than others, but the game allows for strategically discarding over and over to help make up for any issues. While the game does recommend 2-6 players it does work with more, albeit much slower games that can showcase any balance issues. It is always fun to be Prince John and play "beautiful lovely taxes" or play Hercules to Hades board.

A fun game with a little bit of strategy and heavy on theme. Recommend for any disney lovers.

  • Publisher: Ravensburger
  • Player Count: 2-6 players
  • Ages: 10+
