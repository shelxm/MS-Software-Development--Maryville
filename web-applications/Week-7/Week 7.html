About Redwall

Redwall is a series of books written by Brian Jaques. The stories take place in a medival fantasy world populated by anthropomorphic animals and generally centers around an abbey that the series takes it's name from: Redwall. There are 22 books in the Redwall series with animal characters including badgers, hares, moles, otters, foxes, crows, and rats. There have been three animated tv shows, and it was recently announced that Nextflix is developing a movie and tv show based on Redwall.

Synopsis

The Taggerung is the 14th book in the Redwall series by Brian Jaques. A vermin clan led by Sawney Rath believe a prophecy that fortells of the birth of a great warrior, the Taggerung. The clan kidnaps an otter pup from Redwall that bears the mark fortold of in the prophecy. The otter is raised as the clan's own but ends up questioning his reality and leaves to find the truth. Pursued by the clan he meets new friends on the path to finding the answers he seeks.
